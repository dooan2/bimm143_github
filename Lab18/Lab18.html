Q3. Describe what happened after the introduction of the aP vaccine? Do you have a possible explanation for the observed trend?

After the addition of the wP vaccine, pertussis cases went down significantly over the next 25 or so years from 100,000 to near 0. However, after aP vaccine was added, there was a resurgance in cases in the year 2000 on. This could be due to the anti-vax campaign, causing parents to not vaccinate their children.
